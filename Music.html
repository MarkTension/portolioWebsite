Criticality is the state between chaos and order. It is the fertile ground of existence: too much chaos, no harmony. Too much structure, nothing happens.

A zen garden puts nature’s chaos in the context of order as a means to appreciate it. It’s like like a framed curation of it to accentuate its beauty. It is therefore a perfect place to meditate on, and appreciating the synchronistic nature of reality.

Spring Walk Collection is an attempt to build a zen garden of sound. Thereby to develop a deeper appreciation of the critical nature of reality, and its ocean of sound.

This music is an attempt to bring the human mind and nature closer to another though music. To move the mind along the river, meandering between harmony of birdcalls, the unpredictability of rain, and the infinite complexities of clouds.

In the end it’s all a big loving meditation on the play of chaos and order
